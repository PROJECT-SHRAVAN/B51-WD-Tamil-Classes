What is RWD?
    - its an approach to web design that aims to make the web pages render well on a variety of devices and different window / screen sizes.

What is the mobile-first approach?
    - design strategy
    - from where we need to start designing a web page or building a website or an application for mobile devices and then eventually enhance it for the larger screens.
